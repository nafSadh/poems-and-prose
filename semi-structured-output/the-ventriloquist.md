# The Ventriloquist


Sometimes,  
nay oftentimes,  
they tell us:  
*what would everyone think\!*

They never claim as their own  
the opinions  
they force upon us  
in the name of others.

Every time—  
every single time—  
you tell me  
*what would X think,* I know,  
it's only you  
who wants to shove  
your vile constraints down my throat.
