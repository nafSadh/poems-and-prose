# L's Dancefloor

<by: Sadh /><date: Feb 18, 2026 />

Purple.  
Half a crowd.  
No murder yet.

Bodies slow before the beat.  
Neon stick figures freestyle.

I take off the headphones.  
Prepare to jump.  
Beats gonna drop.

I backflip.

The air turns into hot grape syrup  
and everyone gets slapped  
with an ice cream sandwich.

A purple lotus blossoms from the breast  
of the lady on the pole.

An alien octopus.  
Does what an alien octopus does  
to the girl in the lotus.

The crowd gawps.  
Everyone fucks.  
Mushroom, hotdog, fudge.

Well that's the dance floor.
