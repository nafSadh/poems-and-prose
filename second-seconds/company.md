# Company
<date: March 30, 2026 />

Borough's band, dappled drift.  
Silence speaks several storms.  
Musk masks mischievous mirth  
Hushed heart hurries howls  
Presence pours pulpy poise.

Burgundy bar brews bright banter;  
Effervescence evades, even evenings.

Enters   
tall talk's trite tales.

We stare startled   
at the wicked wick.
