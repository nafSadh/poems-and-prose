# a walk?
<by: Sadh /><date: Feb 26, 2026 />

Have you ever  
been on a duo walk, with  
a playlist? Just  
the company, and both  
sharing the same playlist,  
the same path?

I have.  
It was me, and  
my past.  
The silent conversation —  
I went up and down hills,  
looked at my heels, my dusty skin,

I was hungry. Not for  
a sandwich, but paella  
and chai.

I stopped by the treehouse  
and sat with my chai. But  
paella\!  
I didn't have my company.
