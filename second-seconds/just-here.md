# just here


at the end of a day, sorrow  
or sadness  
something, pervasive  
enters my body, my mind

I don't know what it is  
but it enters

every day, almost  
every other day,  
any day,  
when I don't have distractions

when I was a kid  
there was this novelist, from Bangladesh  
Humayun Ahmed  
he had this term:  
"dukkho bilash"  
I don't know how to translate it  
but that happens  
to me  
every day  
whenever  
I  
do  
not  
have  
distractions

I D K

how  
to  
escape  
it

so I accept it

but I want to be in a place  
where sadness is not the norm

I am here

sitting at my kitchen counter  
on my work laptop  
thinking of  
what else I have  
in my life

I don't know  
what to do  
how to do  
why to do

I just keep breathing  
and walking  
and doing

the things that I do

my most common phrase  
"the things that I do"

but is it  
mechanical for me?

where is the spark  
that I had  
some lifetime ago?

can  
I  
get  
that  
spark  
back?

I want it, so badly.  
I need it.

or else\!  
or else?  
I don't know.

just here

typing out a factual diagnosis  
of my un-held emotions

with the hope  
that the very act  
of typing this down  
will  
get me closer

\[end\]
