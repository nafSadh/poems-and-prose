# A Settlement

Where am I?  
Why am I here?   
Why today?

I chose my path,  
I chose my person.  
I chose my poison.

I toiled,  
I laughed,  
I cried,  
I stumbled.

I saw my love in despair,  
I felt the hurt,  
I kept my smile,  
I hid my pain.  
I fought the fight.

And one day,  
I stopped.

The world stopped.  
Love stopped.  
Hurt stopped.

And I looked.  
Up.  
Down.  
Around.  
Within.

And I felt.  
I walked.  
I stopped stopping.

But I felt the void,  
the despair,  
the burn,  
the breakup.

But I also felt a light,  
a hope,  
a breeze,  
a presence.

And so I walked again,  
and asked,  
Why?

I don't need an answer.  
I don't want an answer.  
I just want to ask,  
Why?

And move on.  
Back to my life.  
My self.
