# An Erosion

<by: Sadh /><date: Oct 21, 2025 />

I loved her.  
And she loved me.  
At least I believed  
she loved.

And things shattered.  
I'm lost.  
She's helpless.

I thought she's the strongest,  
but I saw her decay,  
and I saw her turning away.

I craved  
her attention,  
her approval,  
her love.

I sought  
shelter in her.

I kept orbiting,  
around her.

But, we kept hurting  
each other.

It is easy to say  
it was all her fault.  
Easier to say, it was mine.  
She said _it_ was mine.

It wasn’t the stars.  
Something happened  
and then something didn't.

It's just that.  
Nothing grand.  
Nothing special.

That doesn't mean  
It doesn't hurt.  
It hurt when I walked away.  
She died.  
And I died.  
“Us” died.

I know she suffered.  
I loved her.  
And she suffered.  
I failed to save her.  
And she failed to save me.

We both decayed.  
It is just so hard.
