# blunt

I want to feel pain  
because  
feeling pain is better than  
not feeling

and I don't know how to  
inflict any other feeling

so, I want to feel some pain  
a little bit  
just some sensation

I cannot feel the pain

but then I know  
why some people do drugs  
or why  
hurt themselves  
it is not masochism  
it is escape from the nothingness

but I know  
I cannot and would not and shall not  
do that  
that is not the way  
that is the short cut  
that is escape  
without liberation

PS:  
so I scratch my head  
mild scratch  
like when you are puzzled  
because that is what I am  
confuzzled  
with simultaneous overload and absence of feelings
