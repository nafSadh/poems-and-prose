# 8th and 14th

A creased face,  
unnoticed by most  
but held, loved by his Vera.

He took an unsteady step  
onto the L at 8th and 14th —  
a confident, tremoring step;  
grappling the rails and   
his phone.

On his phone,  
Vera as the screensaver.  
On the case,  
Vera in a photograph.  
He held it for many years.  
Vera held the phone, when she was.

The old man;  
the door closes.
